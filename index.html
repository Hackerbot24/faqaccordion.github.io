FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?
Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.
Is Frontend Mentor free?
Yes, Frontend Mentor offers both free and premium coding challenges. The free tier provides access to a wide range of projects, while the premium tier offers additional features and more complex challenges.
Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?
Absolutely! The projects are designed to help you build your portfolio. We encourage you to customize and expand upon the challenges to showcase your unique skills and creativity.
How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?
Frontend Mentor has a supportive community. You can ask for help in the community forum, join our Discord server, or reach out to fellow developers working on the same challenge. Remember, learning often happens when overcoming obstacles!
